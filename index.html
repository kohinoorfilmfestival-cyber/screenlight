About Us

Illuminating Stories That Inspire

The Screen Lights International Film Festival was founded with the vision of creating a stage for filmmakers who dare to dream.

Just like the Kohinoor diamond, we believe every story has brilliance waiting to be revealed. Our talented solutions ensure success across diverse sectors by addressing your unique challenges and opportunities.

We are committed to showcasing diverse voices, innovative storytelling, and cinematic excellence from every corner of the world. Our festival provides a platform where emerging and established filmmakers can share their vision with a global audience.
